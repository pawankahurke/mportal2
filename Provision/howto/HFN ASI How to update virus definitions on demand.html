How to Update Virus Definitions on a System On Demand

Configure the virus definition update action

  1. Log onto your ASI server (https://asiservername/main). The ASI dashboard will be displayed.

  2. On the ASI dashboard, expand the entry for the site where the system whose virus definitions you want to update is located.

  3. In the system list for the site, locate the entry for the system whose virus definitions you want to update, and click on the system name. Management links for the system will be displayed in the right pane

  4. In the right pane, Click on the "manage" link. The Scrip configurator page for the system will be displayed in a new window.

  5. Locate the entry for the virus definition management Scrip for the product installed on the system, e.g. Scrip 12 (Symantec Virus Definition Management), and click on the Scrip name. The selected Scrip's configuration page will be displayed.

  6. Scroll down to the bottom of the Scrip configuration page

  7. Change the selection in the pull-down list to the right of the "Run ... now" Execute button to "machine "<machine name>""

  8. Click on the the "Run ... now" Execute button.

Check the processing of the virus definition update operation

  1. On the page confirming the action you just took, click on the "machines" link found in the "configuration" navigation bar at the upper right end of the page below the "How to" button. The "Configuration - <site name> Machines" page for the site where the system whose virus definitions you just updated will be displayed.

  2. Locate the entry for the system whose virus definitions you just updated. Inspect the entry in the "Changed" column. If it is "No", it means that the command to update the virus definitions on the system, has already been retrieved by the ASI client on the system.

  3. Otherwise, refresh the "Configuration - <site name> Machines" page until the entry in the "Changed" column for the system whose virus definitions you just updated is "No". Please remember that the amount of time it will take for the virus definition action you took to be retrieved by the ASI client on the system whose virus definitions you want to update, won't be greater than the frequency of Scrip 177 (Scrip Configuration Update) execution on the system.

Check the results of the virus definition update action

  1. While still on the the "Configuration - <site name> Machines" page, click on the "ad-hoc query" link in the "events" navigation bar found at the top right-hand corner of the page. The "Ad-hoc Event Query Form" page will be displayed.

  2. In the "Select Additional Search Criteria for Events: " area of the page, select the name of the system whose virus definitions you just updated from the "Machines" pull-down list.

  3. In the "Narrow Your Search (optional): " area of the page, select the entry for the virus definition management Scrip you rand (e.g. Scrip 12 for Symantec) from the "Scrip number" pull-down list

  4. Click on the "Search button. The event log posted by the Scrip after it ran on the system whose virus definitions you updated, will be displayed.

Note

If you want to update virus definitions on all systems at a site or in one group. You should:

  1. Log onto your ASI server (https://asiservername/main)

  2. Click on the "Use extended interface" link on the right side of the ASI Dashboard upper pane, below "user login" and date.

  3. In the "sites" navigation bar, click on the "wizard" link (https://asiservername/main/config/index.php?act=wiz)

  4. Click on the "Scrip configuration" wizard entry. The "Scrip Configuration - Select a Group " page will be displayed.

  5. Select the machine group type (All machines, A single site, A single machine, A group of machines)

If you select "All machines" be extremely careful as the Scrip configuration change you are about to make will affect ALL systems.

  1. Once you have completed the steps for selecting the group of systems you want to update virus definitions on, click on the "Next" button. The "Scrip Configuration - Select Scrip" page will be displayed.

  2. Perform steps 5., 6., and 8. in the “Configure the virus definition update action” section of this document.

  3. Wait for an amount of time equal to the Scrip 177 (Scrip Configuration Update) execution interval for the systems you selected.

  4. Perform the steps in the “Check the results of the virus definition update action” section of this document keeping in mind that if you ran the virus definition update Scrip for a group you defined (i.e. Not a site, or a machine), you will need to check the results of the virus definition update operation for each system in the group individually as you cannot run and ad-hoc event query for a group (except for sites).
