How to Perform Report Section Related Activities

  • Adding a section

  • Copying or editing a section

  • Deleting a section

Adding a section átop

  1. Log onto your ASI server (https://asiservername/main). The ASI dashboard will be displayed.

  2. Click on the "Use extended interface" link on the right side of the ASI Dashboard upper pane, below user login and date. The “Welcome” page will be displayed.

  3. Click on the “sections” link in the “reports” navigation bar found the at upper right-hand corner of any page in the ASI server extended interface. The main “Sections” page will be displayed.

  4. In order to access the add function for the section type of your choice, either:

      • Click on the “add section” link found above the “Search Options” panel. The “ Select a Section Type” page will be displayed. Click on the “Add” button corresponding to the type of section you want to add. The corresponding “Add a Section” page will be displayed, or

      • Click on the section link corresponding to the type of section you want to add, found in the the navigation bar located under the “How to” button, on the right side of the page, giving you access to the pages for asset, event, Microsoft update management (MUM), and summary sections.

  1. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully.

When adding a Microsoft Update Management (MUM) section, please pay special attention to the information contained in the help page for date type parameters (https://asiservername/main/report/datehelp.htm) where the meaning of update status and date types (e.g. install date) is explained.

In the course of defining an event section, when you select event filters, the process is faster and more efficient if you use the “Name Contains” search option parameter in the “Search Options” panel in the “Available Event Query Filters” page reached by clicking on the “Add Existing ...” button to the right of the “event query filters” section of the “Add a Section” page.

  1. After you finish defining a section, click on the “Add” button found at the top and bottom of the page.

Please note that you can access the main “Sections” page,and the “Add a Section” page on the ASI server also via the “sections”, and “add section” inks, respectively, found in the row of links at the top and bottom of the “Reports” page (https://asiservername/main/report/report.php?section=0&act=0).

Copying or editing a report section átop

In order to edit or copy a report section, please perform the following steps:

  1. Log onto your ASI server (https://asiservername/main). The ASI dashboard will be displayed.

  2. Click on the "Use extended interface" link on the right side of the ASI Dashboard upper pane, below user login and date. The “Welcome” page will be displayed.

  3. Click on the “sections” link in the “reports” navigation bar found the at upper right-hand corner of any page in the ASI server extended interface. The main “Sections” page will be displayed.

  4. In order to access the section you want to edit or copy:

      • Enter one or more character strings from the name of the report section you want to copy or edit in the "Name Contains" parameter in the "Search Options" panel on the “Sections” page. If necessary, adjust the value of the “Page Size” parameter in the “Display Options” panel, then click on the “Search” button.

Alternatively,

      • Click on the section link corresponding to the type of section you want to copy or edit (“asset sections”, “event sections”, “MUM sections”, or “summary sections”), found in the the navigation bar located under the “How to” button, on the right side of the page. The corresponding section page will be displayed (“Asset Sections”, “Event Sections”, “Microsoft Update Management Sections” “Summary Sections). Then enter one or more character strings from the name of the section you want to copy or edit in the "Name Contains" parameter in the "Search Options" panel. If necessary, adjust the value of the “Page Size” parameter in the “Display Options” panel, then click on the “Search” button.

  1. Click on the “edit” or “copy” link found in the “Action” column for the section you want to edit or copy. The “Edit a Section” or “Copy a Section” page will be displayed

  1. Follow the on-screen instructions carefully.

  2. Click on the “Update” (if you are editing a section), or “Copy” (if you are copying a section) buttons. Note that if you are editing a section you do not own, the button label in the “Edit a Section” page will be “Add” as you will be adding a copy of the section which you will own.

Deleting a section átop

In order to delete a report section, please perform the following steps:

  1. Log onto your ASI server (https://asiservername/main). The ASI dashboard will be displayed.

  2. Click on the "Use extended interface" link on the right side of the ASI Dashboard upper pane, below user login and date. The “Welcome” page will be displayed.

  3. Click on the “sections” link in the “reports” navigation bar found the at upper right-hand corner of any page in the ASI server extended interface. The main “Sections” page will be displayed.

  4. In order to access the section you want to delete:

      • Enter one or more character strings from the name of the report section you want to copy or edit in the "Name Contains" parameter in the "Search Options" panel on the “Sections” page. If necessary, adjust the value of the “Page Size” parameter in the “Display Options” panel, then click on the “Search” button.

Alternatively,

      • Click on the section link corresponding to the type of section you want to copy or edit (“asset sections”, “event sections”, “MUM sections”, or “summary sections”), found in the the navigation bar located under the “How to” button, on the right side of the page. The corresponding section page will be displayed (“Asset Sections”, “Event Sections”, “Microsoft Update Management Sections” “Summary Sections). Then enter one or more character strings from the name of the section you want to copy or edit in the "Name Contains" parameter in the "Search Options" panel. If necessary, adjust the value of the “Page Size” parameter in the “Display Options” panel, then click on the “Search” button.

  1. Click on the “delete” link found in the “Action” column for the section you want to delete. The “<section type> Section <section name> Deletion” page will be displayed.

  2. Click on the “Yes” link. The “<section type> Section <section name> Deletion” confirmation page will be displayed.
